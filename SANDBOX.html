Our idea

In 1989, in conjunction with one of Venice's most important celebrations, The Redeemer's Festival, a young entrepreneur by the name of Fran Tomasi managed to book the penultimate night of the Pink Floyd's european tour in one of the most peculiar places: the UNESCO site of the Venetian Lagoon.

The floating concert in St. Mark's Basin, proved to be one of the most controversial and historic moments not only for music but for the history of Venice itself.
