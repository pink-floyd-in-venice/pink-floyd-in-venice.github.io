Items

For our project we were asked to find ten items somehow linked to our idea.
These are the ones we chose:

ARTICLE
Newspaper article on the concert's aftermath and the public's outrage at Venice's conditions, retrieved from Europeana.

MAP
A detailed map of the Venice area, with a view on the lagoon where the concert took place, held by the Library of Congress

BOARDGAME
A Venice-themed boardgame on the gloomy future of a future sunken city: players become art collectors looking for valuable treasures.
