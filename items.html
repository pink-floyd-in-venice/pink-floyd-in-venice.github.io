Items and Entities

After building our tailored metadata models we went back to the items and entities we chose and described them with our new model, to take advantage of the different and specific properties. Each item has a thorough description compliant with the standard described for the item's category in our conceptual model. Also, each table can be downloaded from our GitHub repository in open CSV format just by clicking on the name of the item.
